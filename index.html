Tutorial in 1 minute

STxT: a language for building structured text documents, with namespaces and validation.

Documents with namespace

An example document with an Email element from the com.example.email namespace

Email (com.example.email):
	From: John Smith
	To: Mery Adams
	Cc: Keyla Brown
	Title: Project report
	Body Content: 
		Hello Mery!! 
		The book is finished!!

And the definition of the namespace

Namespace: com.example.email
	Email:
		From: (1)
		To: (1)
		Cc: (?)
		Bcc: (?)
		Title: (1)
		Body Content: (1) TEXT
