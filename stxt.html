Our entire website is available in STxT.
Just change *.html to *.stxt
or click on the yellow triangles :-)

We like to take our own medicine ;-)

You’ll see that all pages can be accessed with the extension *.stxt instead of *.html, allowing you to view the original source code of the page. We like free information, and we believe that using STxT is a great way to achieve that. Plus, it didn’t cost us anything to do it. Our original source files were already in STxT!! :-D

By the way, STxT files contain surprises! Check them out, sometimes you'll find something interesting or something that just makes you smile ;-)
